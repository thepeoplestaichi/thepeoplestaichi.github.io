About Melissa:

Melissa has been studying Tai Chi for over 20 years.

Her first (and forever favorite) teacher, Sifu Bryan Knack, has been a major influence in her life and a large part of her passion.

After moving to Spokane WA at age 26, she spotted a little Tai Chi studio.

It took several weeks, and a lot of bravery, but she finally got the courage to walk in the door to inquire about a work-study.

Sifu Bryan helped her fulfill a dream that she'd had since 17 to learn Tai Chi as an adult.

Even today, Sifu Bryan remains a friend and mentor, and has taught her most of what she knows.

Melissa's teaching philosophy, style, and commitment to life-long study are heavily influenced by her studies with him.



Certification:

In 2019, she decided to go to San Diego and certify under Grand Master Dr. Jesse Tsao of Tai Chi Healthways, where she received her teaching certification.

The pandemic came, and shook life up entirely for the whole planet. It was during this time that Melissa decided it made sense to create The People's Tai Chi.

The People's Tai Chi is intended to be inclusive for all, and this is reflected in the pricing structure. Rather than charging for a studio and uniforms, there's a flat sliding fee of $5-$10 per class, based on the student's ability.
